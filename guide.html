Health & Nutrition Guide

This guide provides general nutrition information to help women make informed and healthier food choices. It focuses on common health concerns and balanced eating habits.

PCOS Nutrition

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) requires a balanced diet that helps manage insulin levels and supports hormonal health.

  • Choose low-glycemic foods
  • Include fiber-rich vegetables
  • Limit refined sugars

Anemia & Iron Intake

Anemia is commonly caused by iron deficiency. A balanced diet can help improve iron absorption and energy levels.

  • Include iron-rich foods (leafy greens, lentils)
  • Pair iron with vitamin C sources
  • Avoid tea/coffee immediately after meals

Hormonal Balance

Hormonal health plays a major role in energy levels, mood, and overall well-being.

  • Eat healthy fats (nuts, seeds, oils)
  • Maintain regular meal timings
  • Stay hydrated

Healthy Eating Habits

Simple daily habits can significantly improve overall health and nutrition.

  • Eat balanced meals
  • Control portion sizes
  • Limit processed foods

Note: This guide provides general nutrition information and is not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult a healthcare professional for medical concerns.
