About NutriShe

NutriShe is a women-focused nutrition and diet planning platform created to support healthy and balanced lifestyles. Our goal is to provide simple, safe, and accessible nutrition guidance tailored specifically to women’s unique needs.

Why Women’s Nutrition Matters

Women’s nutritional requirements change with age, lifestyle, and health conditions. Factors such as hormonal changes, iron and calcium needs, and conditions like PCOS or anemia play an important role in shaping dietary requirements.

NutriShe focuses on providing nutrition guidance that is practical, easy to follow, and aligned with everyday lifestyles.

What NutriShe Offers

  • General diet plans based on age, fitness goals, and health conditions
  • Custom diet planning options for personalized guidance
  • Educational nutrition guides and healthy eating tips
  • Frontend-focused, user-friendly experience
